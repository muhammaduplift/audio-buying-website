Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 6, 2025

Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our voice collection service.

Information We Collect

When you participate in our voice collection program, we collect:

  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings of your voice contributions in your chosen language
  • Account Information: Email address, payment details, and basic profile information
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support
  • Usage Data: Recording session times, completion rates, and language selections

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Train and improve AI language models
  • Process payments for your contributions
  • Provide technical support and improve our service
  • Communicate important updates about the program
  • Ensure quality and authenticity of voice data

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption during transmission and storage, secure server infrastructure, and regular security audits. Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Voice recordings may be shared with partner organizations for AI training purposes, but these are anonymized and cannot be linked back to your identity. We may share data with:

  • Payment processors for compensation distribution
  • AI research partners (anonymized voice data only)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of future communications
  • Withdraw consent for voice data usage
  • Request corrections to your information

Data Retention

Voice recordings are retained indefinitely for AI training purposes. Personal account information is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time, though anonymized voice recordings may be retained for research purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect voice data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in different countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the app. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@voicecollection.com
